At FYBS, we welcome everyone with open arms. Let’s grow and explore together!

Who We Are

Cece Wright and Anie Ross, juniors at York, know how hard it can be to find a club that fits their needs. They wanted to explore without being tied down to one club. That’s why they created FYBS, a space for students at York to relax and try new things.
